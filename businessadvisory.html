Our Business Process Services

Payroll Management/Administration

With Benresa Consulting, you can pay salaries to your local and remote employees in a very easy and fast way. You pay us a lump; we handle the rest. We manage your employees’ financial records including salaries, wages, bonuses, deductions, and net pay. These involve; collection of payroll data, computation, disbursement, and reporting of employees’ salaries and related statutory deductions.

Recruitment

We help you hire from the best pool, and reduce the amount of time spent in finding the right talent. With our extensive network combined with our wealth of experience, we find the most talented candidates, who are not just the perfect match, but who are engaged by your vacancy and eager to become a part of your brand. Our approach is to source, recruit and deploy on your behalf as a member of your HR team. In other words, our team of professionals and HR experts will handle your recruitment service tasks from start to finish, giving you the best possible result and engaging the best candidates for you. Our flexible recruitment service is affordable and can save you money compared to maintaining a permanent in-house recruiter/HR person.

Employee Management

Hire Employees or contractors for free with Benresa Consulting. We provide everything you need to hire, onboard, and ensure your remote staff is compliant and productive. Our employee management system helps turn an underperforming employee around or prevent high-performing employees from becoming weak performers. The concept of employee management is more than just making sure that people are doing their jobs; it’s a variety of procedures and strategies that can help you measure, monitor, and interact with the workforce that plays a huge role in your company. With Benresa Consulting, you’re assured of a well-managed, competent, and high-performing workforce.
